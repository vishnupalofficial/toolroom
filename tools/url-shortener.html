Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate age online?

Simply enter your date of birth and the current date in the input fields above, then click the "Calculate Age" button. Our tool will instantly show you your exact age in years, months, and days, along with additional information like your zodiac sign and next birthday countdown.

How do I know my zodiac sign?

Our age calculator automatically determines your Western zodiac sign based on your date of birth. It also shows your Chinese zodiac animal based on the year you were born.

How to find next birthday countdown?

After calculating your age, the tool will show you exactly how many days are left until your next birthday. This countdown is calculated based on your birth date and the current date.
